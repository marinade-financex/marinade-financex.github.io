The Evolution of Yield Farming

Yield farming involves providing liquidity to DeFi protocols in exchange for rewards, typically in the form of tokens. While yield farming has gained popularity, it has also faced criticism due to unsustainable practices and potential risks. Impermanent loss, which occurs when the value of the deposited assets fluctuates compared to the farming rewards, has been a significant concern for farmers. Marinade Finance addresses these issues by implementing sustainable strategies that mitigate risks and optimize returns.
